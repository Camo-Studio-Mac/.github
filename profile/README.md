## Overview

**Camo Studio for Mac** allows users to turn their iPhone or DSLR camera into a high-quality webcam for **macOS**. With precise control over exposure, focus, and white balance, it delivers professional-grade video quality for streaming, video calls, and content creation. The app integrates seamlessly with popular platforms like Zoom, OBS, and Google Meet, providing sharp visuals and reliable performance.

Whether you're using **macOS Ventura**, **Monterey**, or **Sonoma**, **Camo Studio** enhances every video interaction, bringing studio-level clarity and flexibility to your Mac setup.
